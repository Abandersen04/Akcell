Privacy Policy

Last updated: {today}

In short: Grandpa in his bunker does not collect personal information and does not send data off your device.

What the extension does

Grandpa in his bunker replaces selected words in visible page text to create a playful reading experience. All processing happens on your device.

Data we process

  • Your rules (word → replacement): stored via Chrome storage.sync or storage.local so they persist between visits and (optionally) sync across your own devices.

What we do not do

  • No collection or sharing of personal data. No analytics. No ads.
  • No reading or modifying of form fields (input, textarea) or editable content (contentEditable).
  • No network requests to the developer.

Retention & deletion

Your rules remain until you remove them. Use “Clear all” in the extension popup, or uninstall the extension to remove everything.

Permissions

  • storage — to store your rules/preferences.
  • Site access — runs as a content script to display replacements on pages.
